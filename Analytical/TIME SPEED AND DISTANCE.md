### Core Formulas

- **Speed = Distance/Time**
- **Distance = Speed × Time**
- **Time = Distance/Speed**
- **Average Speed** = Total Distance/Total Time

### Unit Conversions

- **km/hr to m/s**: Multiply by 5/18
- **m/s to km/hr**: Multiply by 18/5

### Relative Speed

- **Opposite directions**: Relative speed = S₁ + S₂
- **Same direction**: Relative speed = |S₁ - S₂|

### Trains Problems

- Time to cross a pole/person = Length of train/Speed
- Time to cross a platform = (Length of train + Length of platform)/Speed
- Two trains crossing each other = (L₁ + L₂)/(S₁ + S₂) [opposite] or (L₁ + L₂)/|S₁ - S₂| [same direction]

### Boats and Streams

- **Downstream speed** = Speed in still water + Stream speed
- **Upstream speed** = Speed in still water - Stream speed
- **Speed in still water** = (Downstream + Upstream)/2
- **Stream speed** = (Downstream - Upstream)/2

### Practice Problems

**Example 1**: A train 150m long passes a pole in 15 seconds. Find speed. **Solution**: Speed = 150/15 = 10 m/s = 10 × (18/5) = 36 km/hr

**Example 2**: A man rows 20 km downstream in 2 hours and upstream in 4 hours. Find his speed in still water. **Solution**: Downstream speed = 20/2 = 10 km/hr Upstream speed = 20/4 = 5 km/hr Speed in still water = (10+5)/2 = 7.5 km/hr
