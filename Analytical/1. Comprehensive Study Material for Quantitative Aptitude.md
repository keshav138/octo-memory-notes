
## EXAM TIPS AND STRATEGIES

### Time Management

- Spend maximum 2 minutes per question
- Attempt easy questions first
- Mark difficult questions for review

### Common Shortcuts to Remember

1. Square of numbers ending in 5: (n×(n+1)) followed by 25
    - 25² = (2×3)25 = 625
2. Percentage to fraction conversions (memorize common ones)
3. Tables up to 20, squares up to 30, cubes up to 15
4. Remember 2ⁿ values: 2¹⁰ = 1024, 2⁵ = 32, 2⁸ = 256

### Practice Strategy

- Solve at least 50 problems per topic
- Take timed mock tests
- Analyze mistakes and weak areas
- Create formula sheets for quick revision

### Mental Math Tips

- Use approximation when exact values aren't needed
- Break complex calculations into simpler parts
- Use options for back-calculation in MCQs
- Eliminate obviously wrong options first
