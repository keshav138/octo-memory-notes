### Why RAG Specifically

This is a case where RAG is genuinely the right tool:

- Documents are too long for a context window
- The answer is always grounded in the document — hallucination is unacceptable
- New filings come out quarterly — you can't fine-tune for every update
- Source attribution ("this came from page 34") adds trust
